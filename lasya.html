Lasya

With an exquisite blend of rhythm and poise, a touch of innovation, and a footfall of more than a thousand, Lasya represents the dance exponent of Pravega, and displays the delicate balance of passion and grace in our life.

What happens?

The competition will consist of two rounds, Video Preliminaries in which teams will send videos of their auditions for screening, and the Finals in which the top teams will face-off on the grand stage in Pravega.

How do I register?

For participation in this dance extravaganza, interested teams can register online, the links of which are up. Get that audience grooving to your beats and swaying to your rhythm.

What do I get?

The winners of last year, SJCE Mysore, mesmerized the audience, and bagged the lucrative first prize. Besides the prize money, the winning team is sure to win the hearts of many. So, get ready to dance your way into their hearts.
