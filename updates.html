    Pronites

    We present to you the best talents of the entertainment industry to leave an everlasting impact, and bring to a perfect end the amazing experience Pravega is.

  • PIS

    Pravega Innovation Summit

    Pravega Innovation Summit is the flagship event of Pravega 2018, IISc, which is an initiative to bring researchers, students and investors, together, providing a platform for networking and exchange of ideas to foster collaboration.

  • Hackathon

    Hackathon

    Utilize the latest and greatest in machine learning to help computers understand emotions in our Hackathon!

  • Workshops

    Workshops

    Ever wanted to learn something of great value, which would help your career in the future? Or maybe you wanted to learn something fun. Either way, take your pick from the wide variety of workshops in Pravega. We guarantee that at the end of the day, you would have learned something new.

  • Quizzes

    Quizzes

    Delve into the world of quizzing in Pravega and pit your brains against others for glory and magnificent rewards!

  • Biology

    Biology

    Biology gives you a brain, life turns it into a mind. Exercise that mind to find the culprit of a murder in Whodunit, and to paint up a masterpiece in PECOSA.

  • Chemistry

    Chemistry

    Never trust atoms, they make up everything. Make up your artwork using mysterious chemical concoctions, and then explain the CheMystery behind the masked magician's acts.

  • Mathematics

    Maths

    Mathematics is a place where you can do things, which you cannot do in the real world. Explore this idea, as you strategize for the Daedalian auction and learn the Tricks of Trade.

  • Decoherence

    Be a part of the biggest physics competition at the Undergraduate level. Display your skills and battle it out against the best the country has to offer. Get a chance to apply your knowledge of physics to real research problems because nature is not as simple as our schooling might lead us to believe.

  • Physics

    Physics

    No one undertakes research in Physics with the intention of winning a prize. They do it for the joy of discovering something no one knew before. Explore this joy while experiencing Decoherence, and shoot a Vacuum Cannon while you’re at it.

  • Engineering

    Engineering

    Engineering is the closest thing to magic existing in the world. Use that magic to create robots and tanks, and enjoy the same while shooting your enemies down in a game of laser tag.

  • Lasya

    Lasya

    Dance is magical, something that sets you free. It is dreaming with your feet, and exhibiting your passion. Display your own passion in this dance extravaganza, and leave the stage on fire.

  • BoB

    Battle of Bands

    A band is not proud because it performs well, it performs well because it is proud. Put your pride and that of your band on the line, in the Battle of the Bands, and blow the roof off!

  • Proscenium

    Proscenium

    Acting is everyone’s favorite second-job. Change your look and your attitude, and you can be anyone. In Proscenium, we believe an ounce of action is worth a pound of words. So stop explaining yourself, Act!

  • Open Mic

    Open Mic

    Great talents need a platform to express themselves. Pravega's Open Mic provides that platform. Step up to the mic and don't be afraid to say "This is my ability; this is who I am"!

  • AAI

    Airbus Aeromodelling Initiative

    Not everyone is born with a gold spoon in their mouths; the world is filled with people who don't have many privileges which we consider routine in our lives. Pravega in conjunction with Airbus aims to help such students while sparking their interest in the field of aeromodelling in the Airbus Aeromodelling Initiative.

  • Sponsors
